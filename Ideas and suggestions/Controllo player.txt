D-Pad:
Left and right arrow the character rotate on its Y-axis.
Up and down arrow the character advances or recedes.
Face buttons:
Bottom button the character jump.
Right button the character attack.
Left button the character use the selected item (if available).
Up button the character use the selected magic (if available).
Shoulder buttons:
Top-right: next magic.
Top-left: previous magic.
Bottom-right: next item.
Bottom-left: previous item.
